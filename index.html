Attribute Bars: Can be clicked and the track map will show that attribute for the selected driver
Timeline Rect: Can be click to show current timestap where you click, can also use arrowkeys to jump one data point at a time

Dots are colored based on the actual colors of the team, and first driver has black outline, meanwhile second driver has yellow outline
Note: Some tracks do not have a track outline and thus only the actual raceline will be rendered: With outline:
